Zirui Wu

Zirui Wu received his B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing Institute of Technology, China. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree in robotics and autonomous systems (ROAS) at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou campus), supervised by Prof. Jie Song. From 2022, he has been a research intern at the DISCOVER Lab, Institute for AI Industry Research, Tsinghua University. His researchs focus on neural implicit represenations, 3D computer vision, robotics and autonomous driving.
